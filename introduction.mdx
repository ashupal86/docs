# Welcome to the Devinit Frontend Guide!

This documentation will help you understand how to use the Devinit platform to create, configure, and manage your subdomains. Our goal is to provide you with clear and concise instructions to make your experience as smooth as possible.

## What You Can Do

With Devinit, you can:

*   **Instantly check the availability** of your desired subdomains.
*   **Register new subdomains** quickly through our integrated checkout process.
*   **Configure DNS records** (like A, CNAME, TXT, AAAA) for your active subdomains directly from your profile.
*   **Manage all your purchased subdomains** in one convenient place.
*   **Clear (delete) subdomain registrations** if you no longer need them.

## How to Use This Guide

This guide is organized into several sections to help you find the information you need:

*   **Getting Started**:
    *   [Creating a Subdomain](./subdomains/creating-subdomain.mdx): Step-by-step instructions on how to find and register a new subdomain.
    *   [Configuring & Verifying Your Subdomain](./subdomains/verifying-subdomain.mdx): Learn how to set up DNS records for your subdomain to point it to your desired service or IP address. This step is crucial for making your subdomain live.
*   **Help & Support**:
    *   [Contact Us](./support/contact-us.mdx): Find out how to get in touch with our support team if you encounter any issues or have questions.

We recommend starting with the "Creating a Subdomain" guide if you're new to the platform.

If you run into any trouble, please check the troubleshooting sections within each guide or refer to our "Contact Us" page.
